      Help / About

      Welcome!

      This app provides Text to Speech (TTS), Speech to Text (SST), and translation features in one place.

      On Mobile

      Select a voice and language from the dropdown menus. Tap "Start Recognition" to begin speech input, or type text for TTS. Use "Speak" to hear your typed text.

      On Desktop

      Use Chrome or Edge for best results. Choose a voice from the "Voice" dropdown. If the selected voice doesn't support your chosen language, we'll fetch audio from Google's TTS for that language.

      History

      All TTS and SST entries are stored locally. You can search, download (as JSON), or clear them.

      Settings

      Toggle Dark Mode, adjust font size, or pick a new font family. Your preferences are saved automatically.

      Language Support

      Enjoy exploring different languages (including Indian languages like Tamil, Telugu, Kannada). If a matching local voice doesn't exist, we use an online fallback TTS.
